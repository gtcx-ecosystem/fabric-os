# Brand

Visual brand guardrails for generated media.

## Direction

Use this track to record Fabric OS-specific source prompts, review notes, usage constraints, and approved asset patterns for the AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform, deployment, and independent assurance control plane.

## Storage

Generated binaries belong under `platform/assets/generated/media/` and must be indexed in `machine/media/library.manifest.json` before use in product, docs, presentations, or campaigns.

## Review Rules

- Generated assets are illustrative unless explicitly sourced as product screenshots or approved evidence.
- Do not create fake credentials, certificates, seals, badges, QR codes, official forms, or partner proof.
- Do not use generated assets as audit evidence, compliance proof, identity proof, or inspection evidence.
- Record generator, prompt, intended usage, owner, and review status in the media manifest.
